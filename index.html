Teacher for over 9 years. I mainly teach professionals. They would often meet me in the middle of a very dense day, so I've always felt my job's not merely giving them a chance of practicing the language, or learning some new grammar and vocabulary, but living the language by immersing into it, through material and discussions that are truly of interest to them. This translates into being empathetic and always up to date, dynamic and positive.
